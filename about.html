Our Story

From a Small Startup to a National Leader

NexaLink Telecom was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate engineers and telecom veterans who saw an opportunity to transform the way people connect to the internet. Starting from a small office in San Francisco with just 15 employees, we launched our first fiber optic network in the Bay Area, serving a modest 500 customers.

What set us apart from the beginning was our unwavering commitment to speed, transparency, and customer satisfaction. While other providers imposed data caps and bandwidth throttling, we offered unlimited, full-speed internet from day one. This approach resonated with customers, and word spread quickly.

By 2015, we had expanded to 10 major cities and crossed 10,000 subscribers. Our investment in fiber optic infrastructure paid off as the demand for high-speed internet surged with the rise of streaming, remote work, and smart home devices. In 2019, we launched our mobile division, offering 4G and 5G-ready SIM cards and data plans, making us a complete telecom solution provider.

Today, NexaLink serves over 50,000 subscribers across 45+ cities, employs 800+ dedicated professionals, and has been recognized as the "Best ISP" for three consecutive years by the National Telecom Awards. Our $200M investment in next-generation infrastructure ensures we stay ahead of the curve for years to come.
