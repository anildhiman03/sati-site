Easy Moving

Connection Relocation

Moving to a new home or office shouldn't mean losing your internet connection or dealing with complicated re-registration processes. NexaLink's Connection Relocation service ensures a smooth, seamless transition to your new address. Your existing plan, speed tier, and billing remain exactly the same.

Simply schedule a relocation through your account dashboard or call our support team. We'll coordinate with you on a convenient date and time for disconnection at the old address and installation at the new one. In most cases, we can complete the entire process within the same day, minimizing your offline time.

  • Same plan, same speed, same price at your new address
  • Same-day relocation in most metro areas
  • Flexible scheduling — choose your preferred date & time
  • No relocation fee for customers with 12+ months tenure
  • Reduced fee ($25) for newer customers
  • Complete equipment setup at new location
  • Network optimization at new premises

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services, plans, installations, and more.

What internet speeds do you offer? +

We offer fiber optic broadband plans with speeds ranging from 100 Mbps (Starter) to 10 Gbps (Enterprise). All speeds are symmetrical, meaning your upload speed matches your download speed. There is no throttling or speed reduction at any time, regardless of usage.

How long does a new connection installation take? +

In metro areas, we complete new installations within 24 hours of order confirmation. For suburban and rural locations, our SLA is 48 hours. Our technician will coordinate with you to schedule a convenient 2-hour installation window.

Is there a contract or lock-in period? +

No! NexaLink operates on a month-to-month basis. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time without penalties. We believe in earning your loyalty through great service, not contracts.

Do you offer business/enterprise plans? +

Yes! Our Enterprise plan offers up to 10 Gbps speeds, dedicated account management, SLA guarantees (99.99% uptime), DDoS protection, unlimited static IPs, and priority support. We also offer custom solutions for large organizations — contact our sales team for a tailored quote.

Can I keep my phone number when switching to NexaLink Mobile? +

Absolutely! We support number porting from all major carriers. The porting process typically completes within 2 hours, and you'll experience minimal to zero service interruption during the switch. Our team handles all the paperwork for you.

What happens if I experience service issues? +

Contact our 24/7 support team via phone (1-800-123-4567), live chat, or email. Our AI-powered diagnostics can resolve 70% of issues remotely within minutes. For on-site issues, a technician is dispatched with a guaranteed 4-hour response time in metro areas. We provide real-time status updates throughout the process.

Do you provide international roaming for mobile SIM? +

Yes! NexaLink Mobile offers international roaming in 120+ countries. We have competitive roaming packages for data, calls, and SMS. You can activate roaming through your account dashboard or our mobile app before you travel. Daily, weekly, and monthly roaming bundles are available.
